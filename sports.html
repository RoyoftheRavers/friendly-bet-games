Football

Football fans have no shortage of games to choose from to play against their friends. Whether it’s pitting their wits against mates in a Prediction Competition, organising a Sweepstake for the office/work crowd or challenging colleagues in a Last Man Standing contest, there is plenty of fun to be had for both devoted followers and casual fans of the beautiful game!

Competitions available for football include Last Man Standing in the Premier League and league of Ireland, as well as Sweepstakes for the League of Ireland. See below for details of upcoming fixtures on the official websites.

Horse Racing

Whether you’re a fanatical stable expert or a ‘bandwagoner’ Grand National/Cheltenham fan, Friendly Bet has more than enough horse racing competitions to whet your appetite! Friendly Bet offers you the chance to organise Sweepstakes online in the Grand National and Cheltenham gold cup. There are also both prediction competitions in both of these festivals as well as some great Grade 1 competition offerings in racing in Ireland, France and beyond!

Competitions available for horse racing include Prediction Competitions and Sweepstakes for the Aintree and Cheltenham festivals in the UK, as well as Leopardstown and Punchestown festivals in Ireland. There are also offerings for the Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe in France and the Kentucky Derby in the US. See below for details of upcoming racing festivals on the official websites.

Cheltenham Festival https://cheltenhamfestivaluk.com/race-schedule/ Aintree Festival https://www.racingfixtures.co.uk/courses/aintree.php

Golf

Golf fans can now challenge their friends, family or work associates in all four major Professional Golf championships throughout the calendar year. There are both Sweepstakes and Prediction Competitions available in all of these tournaments so get studying the form ahead of tee time!

Golf tournaments available on Friendly Bet include Sweepstakes and Prediction Competitons for the British Open Championship, the US Masters, the US Open and the US PGA Championship. See below for details of upcoming fixtures on the official websites.

PGA Tournaments https://www.pgatour.com/tournaments/schedule.html

GAA

GAA fans looking to test their skills against their fellow fans or teammates will be delighted to hear they can now compete in prediction competitions for the GAA championships in both Football and Hurling. Sweepstakes are also available in both codes – with a ‘Super 8’ sweep in Football running alongside a Quarter final sweep in hurling meaning the excitement will be enhanced all the way to All-Ireland final day!

Competitions available in GAA are based on the Leinster, Munster, Ulster, Connacht and All-Ireland championships in football and the Leinster, Munster, and All-Ireland championships in hurling. See below for details of upcoming fixtures on the official GAA website.

GAA Fixtures https://www.gaa.ie/fixtures-results/

Rugby

Rugby enthusiasts can now attempt to get one over their fellow fans by competing at forecasting results at both club and international level! Casual rugby fans can get involved too with Sweepstakes offered in the business end of the biggest tournaments including the World Cup and Champions Cup.

Competitions available for rugby include Prediction Competitions for the Pro 14 and Champions Cup for clubs, as well as Six Nations and World Cup Sweepstakes. See below for details of upcoming fixtures on the official websites.

Champions Cup https://www.epcrugby.com/champions-cup/matches/fixtures-and-results/ Six Nations https://www.sixnationsrugby.com/
